This is the readme for the model associated with the paper:

Volo MD, Romagnoni A, Capone C, Destexhe A (2019) Biologically Realistic Mean-Field Models of Conductance-Based Networks of Spiking Neurons with Adaptation. Neural Comput 31:653-680
http://cognet.mit.edu/pdfviewer/journal/neco_a_01173

This archive was contributed by M di Volo.

Launch the program MF_simulation.py

The program simulates the Mean field model and its response to external stimuli with exponential rise and decay, as in Fig. 3A1 of di Volo et al. Neural Comp. 2019.
